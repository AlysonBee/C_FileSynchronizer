What is it


This project is about making a faster, easier way to prototype chatbots and dialogue for creative projects.

It's as simple as writing a short story or blog post. No tagging or dialogue trees

All the info you write will be integrated into its responses naturally.

For Creatives


Populate your digital world with NPCs or build a QandA bot.


Download your created bot to run locally or point to it's unique API endpoint to retrieve responses.


For Contributors


Contribute your bot to the network and offer up it's responses and knowledge to other bots, making each creation more immersive.


This current POC is running a TimeKeeping bot and CakeChat.
